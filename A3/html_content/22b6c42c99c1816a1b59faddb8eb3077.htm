Griffin Emblem


Griffin Emblem is a brand name that symbolized the protector of hidden treasure and wealth. This mythical creature made by the combination of lion and hawk is about the treasure, specifically the high quality factored in the mechanical watch brand. Fine finishing yet fashionable with a magnificent oscillating from the balance wheel, a special group of people pays for this kind of front view skeleton in movement. A Japanese made high quality mechanism using stainless steel gives a stable performance and high sensitivity. Accuracy is none other than the result despite years of grinding and grinding.

Nothing beats an affordable and fashionable traditional technology, combined with professionalism. Clear, clean lines design suits all occasions, be it grandeur or a summer outdoor celebration. Elegance and beauty bestowed the wearer. This year, Mr. Ricardo Pinto who is the champion of Mr. France 2014 is the ambassador of this unique Griffin Emblem.

Exquisite workmanship can be seen from its intricate carvings. Griffin Emblem watches also wear flowers manufacturing process. This makes the series movements ever so beautiful. The American Society for Testing and Material (ASTM) 316L(Low carbon) metal promises a low impact on our human skin. Griffin Emblem watches are also made using an advanced technology of frosting and polishing, a fine and clear strong three-dimensional appearance with an advanced leather strap to make this watch a comfort for any wearer.

For business deals, Griffin Emblem provides a global sales and service at very competitive pricing at everywhere around the world. Find out more about the international guarantee as a Griffin Emblem timepiece owner now.
