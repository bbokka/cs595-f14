MitoAction Energy Walk & 5K

Join us on Sunday, Sept. 14, 2014 at DCR's Mother's Rest in South Boston, MA for the 10th annual MitoAction Energy Walk & 5K!

 REGISTRATION IS OPEN!


Come together on Sunday, Sept. 14, 2014 to raise awareness about mitochondrial disease and kick off Mito Awareness Week!

The walk & 5K features a wonderful raffle, great Mito merchandise, fun & games for the kids, and refreshments. It's a wonderful opportunity to meet other families, renew friendships, and make new friendships! 


Are you a RUNNER?  This is a flat, beautiful course along the water! Start training and bring your friends.
To sign up to volunteer at the event and have a lot of fun, click here.

Don't live in the area, but want to host your own MitoAction Energy Walk?  Contact us today and let us help you!
