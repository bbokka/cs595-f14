My Future Husband!

I'm hoping to inspire and touch people in some way. - August Alsina

 
  
 
Hey, everyone! Kayla here...again. Sorry I haven't posted in a minute, school is driving me crazy. All these projects and all this homework, and we just got done with the first week of school. But, that doesn't matter right now. I came to express my love for this wonderful man that I love so dearly, yet he doesn't know I exist, and that is my business!! But, he will one day. Trust me!
 
If you know me personally, or know of me, you will know that I am a die hard fan for August Alsina, and I have been for quite some time. Most people (girls), from where I live now, only like him because of the handsome looks God took his time to bless him with, but I will tell you the real reason I love August...this may take a minute.
  
Well, I just remember one morning, I was getting ready for school. I was in my room getting dressed, and MTV Jams was on my tv (I watch it almost every morning before school). This horrible Fabolous song had just went off, and I was thinking about turning it off and playing my own music, but something told me not too. Thank God I didn't! All I heard was this guy kept chanting the words, I love it. The beat was really catchy, and the guy was oh so sexy! I just could not stop staring at the tv.
 
When the song finally went off, I caught his name flashing across the tv. It said, August Alsina. To tell the truth, I questioned the name. August Alsina? I thought it was a joke, to be honest. But, I rocked with the song. His voice was nice, but I needed to hear more before I really judged him.
 
With that said, when I got to school, I looked him up. I read about him, and learned some things that really stood out to me - like, the fact that his brother was killed, and how he was homeless. I really began to have sympathy for August, and my love for him grew fast and deep. It's not everyday we find a new R&B artist, or even a rap artist, who shares their struggle so that listeners can relate. All these days, we have artists telling us what they have, and it's really like a slap in the face, because not all of us can say we have fancy cars, huge houses, and jewelry and clothes to die for. Instead, August tells us where he came from, how he grew up, hard life experiences he went through, and at just 16 years old, I can relate to him - in more ways than one.
 
Besides the good looks (which are a plus to me loving him), August speaks the truth. One thing he always says in interviews is, no one will ever rob me of my honesty, and I respect that 100%. But, that's also a reason why some people see him as a threat. August keeps it real 24/7, and some people confuse it with him being angry and mean - that's not the case. If you feel that way, then maybe he's just too real for you to handle, and you should find someone else who fits your real description.
 
I tell you, the minute I got home, I hopped on my computer and downloaded EVERYTHING he made. Mixtapes and all, and I just really sat there and listened to his music. I shed a few tears here and there, and now each song he's made has a little part of me. I've watched every interview, and I just vibe with him. He gives off a spirit that I love, and for that, I'll always be a fan of him.
 
If you're not into August Alsina, just take the time to listen to his music before you judge. I feel like we should all do that will all music artists. You never know, you might just find someone who you really dig. I will say, his attitude can be....hard, at times, but he's a cool guy. I really hope I get the chance to meet him one day and just share how his music has helped me with my own life experiences.
 
 
Liked this blog post? Hit me up, and let me know. While you're here, Please take the time and follow me on my social networks (the links are below). Have a blessed day!
 
 
Love, Always!
 
 
Kayla :)

Well, Hello!

This is actually my first post, so I just thought I'd start off by introducing myself and tell you why I'm here. 



My name is Kayla, but my family and close friends call me Kay. Simple. I'm 16 years old, and I'm just living life. My dream is to become a Television and Radio Talk Show Host, an actress, and an entrepreneur. A lot, right? Well, it is, truth be told, but I'm striving to make all of those things come true. During my free time, I love to write on my Wattpad site (you should definitely check out my work), listen to music, and enjoy time with the people I love most. Now, I'm getting into the blogging thing. I'm a very simple person; I don't ask for much, but I'm very unique.

I believe I'm very different than most teens around my age group - I'm not seen at parties every weekend, I've never smoked weed, and I'm a virgin. I believe life has more to offer than just throwing it away every chance you get. There's so much more than just the simple things, and I want to be successful. Being out in the streets will not get me there, so I try to stay away as far as I can. I wish every teen could have the same mindset as myself, but, unfortunately, they can't.

On this blog, I'll be talking about everything that comes to mind - personal issues, everyday life, and just things I feel like getting off my chest or anything that comes to mind. Feel free to share your thoughts with me, I'd love to hear them. Also, please keep everything negative away from this blog. I don't affiliate myself with that, and I'd appreciate if you keep the negative comments away. I hear enough unwanted comments in my life already, I'd hate to come here and have to deal with it. You don't have to be here.

I'll try and post when I can. I just downloaded the app, so expect to hear A LOT from me. I talk too much (so I've been told), and I type even more!


Love, Always!



Kayla :)
