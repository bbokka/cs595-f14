Welcome to Honey Pot Bed & Breakfast Guest House, Umhlanga Rocks - where your stay will be a unique experience.

Your hosts Heather and Bob offer first class European style ensuite B&B accommodation coupled with peace and privacy for your holiday or business trip.

At this owner-managed guest house you can enjoy either full English or Continental breakfast served in the breakfast room overlooking the pool. Set in a private garden, Honey Pot is an ideal base being just 1km from the fabulous beach and village of Umhlanga, an upmarket coastal location. Umhlanga has excellent restaurants, shops and amenities for corporate travellers and gives holidaymakers easy access to some exciting water sports and land-based adventures and experiences..

Honey Pot is only 15km north of Durban and 13km from King Shaka International Airport.

During your stay at Honey Pot you will have free access to:
• Swimming pool
• Outside lounge and bar area
• Portable braai (bbq) for use at the poolside or outside your room
• Onsite business facilities including fax, word processing service, free WiFi and internet computer
 
Other available facilities on request for a nominal fee are:
• Laundry service
• Picnic baskets
• Airport transfers
• Day trips and tours
• Car hire through a reputable local company or your choice of the larger international companies
