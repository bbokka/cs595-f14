Future Update

Okay, so in about a year (give or take) I’m gonna be finally moving into my own place. Little apartment with a few things and everything will probably be simple. I’ll be finishing up school and looking towards moving out somewhere to finally start working with whatever I decide to do. 

It’s all exciting, and I know for a fact that the next 2 years of my life will be constantly changing and improving. I’ll be happy to finally get away, and meet new people, but I’ll be even happier to hopefully enjoy this change with the people who mean most to me right now, they know who they are. 

Nevertheless, even though the future seems so bright, I’m avoiding a common mistake of looking too far ahead and expecting anything. Each new day this summer, fall, and the rest of these two years have to be lived very specifically. Any time that is not spent working on apps, repairs, or other business, will be spent with my babygirl, Mia, and my best friends. I don’t know where I visit, sleep, eat, or do until most likely a day before, if that, but that is the way I want it to be. 

On a side note, I think one of the reasons I’m so excited for Summer this year is Mia. She’s honestly a person who is not just someone who is a blast to spend my time with, but also someone who improves the things I do, and motivates me to do better with everything. She’s my best friend, and a person I can always count on, just like a team. We both look out for each other, and that’s something that is priceless. It’s going to be fun to see where we go and where life takes us from day to day. 

I’ll attempt to put up more and more of my summer, and whatever comes after that. You’ll see the people I spend time with, meet, places I go, eat at, and whatever else I think is cool enough to share along the way.
