Payers in the heart.

Hello, Brand new week, no Monday blues but choose to feel refresh, renewed. Still counting down to the day I finish O level. Feeling a little stress with people asking hows my preparation for OLevel. Still a lot to be done with not much time left. But just gotta keep moving on, giving the best I can ever give.

Was thinking what to update or share and "prayers in the heart" came to my mind. Sometimes I'm too sad, too down and too disappointed and have no words to describe it. Have you felt the same way before ? Not sure about You but this few days has been feeling really tired and hasn't gotten enough rest. Have a good week people ! :)

Friday, 23 May 2014

Don't forget who You are.

Hi. so I kind of deleted my past post and started with a brand new one. Been long since I last blogged, glad to be back.
O levels in just less than 5 months. Seriously wished that O level was a "yesterday" thing. Its a killer though A levels is worst but I just cant wait for it to past However I'm actually quite scared of failing... But what to do ? just gotta do the best and fight to the finish.

Had a great day jamming with church friends and really happy to be back holding on to that microphone even though it was just for a few hours singing for fun. Long Long day...

This few days one thing that really caught me thinking was Don't forget Who You Are. Probably I have forgotten what I used to like and enjoy doing or maybe I should phrase it I have stopped doing what I truly enjoy and have changed a little inside. Feeling of a stranger within me. But I wanna be who God wanna me be and I think that is the meaning behind Don't forget Who You Are. Don't forget What God has Called You to be probably. Hopefully this feeling of having a stranger in me would soon go away and i would realise that it is not a stranger but God shaping me.

I'm finishing the book " Becoming myself : embracing God's dream for You".. It was a good read. Cant wait to start the new one.

This is one of the song that I have been listening to. Hope it blesses Your heart.
