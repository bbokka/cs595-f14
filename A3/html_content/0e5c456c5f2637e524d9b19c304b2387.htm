Earn Rewards from CRYSTAL LIGHT: Miranda Lambert Platinum Points

by Freebies4Mom on October 3, 2014 3:56 pm

Sign-up for the new Miranda Lambert Platinum Points Rewards program using the promo code mirandafm to start with 30 points. With just one purchase of CRYSTAL LIGHT you’ll be rewarded with a free song download of Miranda Lambert’s Automatic (acoustic). You’re on your way to earning free rewards when you remember to scan your receipt when you buy CRYSTAL LIGHT!  I just earned a free Miranda Lambert Platinum CD  by submitting my receipt for eight CRYSTAL LIGHT products!

You’ll find CRYSTAL LIGHT products in the “Drink Mixes” aisle at Walmart. If you buy just one CRYSTAL LIGHT product (100 points), you will earn a free song download of Miranda Lambert’s Automatic (acoustic). I bought 8 packages of the new CRYSTAL LIGHT Pure Sugar Free On The Go (800 points) and I’ll be rewarded with a free Miranda Lambert Platinum CD. I love to throw these On The Go packets in my purse so I can enjoy a flavorful drink while I’m out and about! Don’t worry about having enough time to make your purchases, because this rewards program will last until December 31, 2014. So you can accumulate points for your CRYSTAL LIGHT purchases all year long. You might even save enough to be rewarded with a free Miranda Lambert Signed Guitar (8,000 points) Which Platinum Points Reward do you want to earn?

Buy CRYSTAL LIGHT, Get Rewarded! #CrystalLightWM

It’s easy to claim your free Platinum Points Reward after you purchase Crystal Light products at Walmart where prices start at $2.18. This offer ends on December 31, 2014. Here’s what you need to do:

1. Buy CRYSTAL LIGHT products at Walmart. Look in the aisle with the Drink Mixes.

2. Register at EarnPlatinumPoints.com on your desktop or mobile and enter your name, phone number, email address and password to register. Enter this promo code mirandafm to start with 30 points!

3. Snap a photo of your receipt. It’s super easy using your mobile phone – just make sure you have good lighting and you’ve captured the entire receipt.

4. Submit your receipt. You’ll receive a text message and email that your receipt has been received – and then another one when it’s been approved.

5. Order your Reward. After your receipt has been received and confirmed, you can order your free Reward with the Platinum Points you just earned. Enjoy your reward!
