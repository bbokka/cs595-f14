Lonely Nights

As darkness looms and the air gets colder
my heart longs for a shoulder to lean on
an ear to listen to my 3 am thoughts
and an arm to hold me when I shiver

shuffling in my bed tormented anxiety attacks me
nothing among me but the sound of the crickets outside my door
loneliness takes over 
not a person in sight to keep me sane 
or to keep me away from the thoughts lingering in my head

I stare into the darkness of my room expectant
as if I'm waiting on someone to show up
someone to tell me I'm not alone

confused and bored I scroll through my messages 
the bad...the ones I dont want to recall having 
the good...the ones that gives me some hope
that all my nights won't always be this lonely

I smile reliving those moments where our conversations go wild
and my heart flutters to the sound of your voice
snuggling closer to my pillow I imagine being curled up in your arms
falling asleep to the sound of your heartbeat and your arms wrapped around me.

Crooked Smile

There was this petite young lady always nicely dressed a beauty I would say
Always walking with her head towards the ground
as if she was searching for something below
from time to time she would look up and down like a bobble-head doll
mainly because she didn't want to bounce into anyone or anything

Her face nicely sculpted with neat flared nose
and lips red like full ripe cherries with a pout so tempting to kiss
there was something missing from all that beauty she possessed
.....her smile
she sat in a crowd of laughing people with a frown on her face

Why does she hide that smile with a frown?
Observing that beauty from across a crowded room
I saw sadness in her eyes
sadness with a touch of delight
as if she wanted to smile but her lips were glued together

Why does she hide that smile with a frown?
I thought to myself as I stared intently at her from across the room
She looked quite uncomfortable like she didn't belong
always shifting or hiding that beautiful face when the camera lights flashed
A face like that why didn't she want her pictures taken?

Curious to know about this petite beauty I introduced myself
she looked at me with confusion in her eyes and that frown on her face
a mumbled reply as if her lips were still glued together
and a shyness that was both cute and confusing
I wondered why does she hide that smile with a frown?

Getting to know her I realized the source of her awkwardness
Why she covered her mouth when she laughed
why she walked around with a constant frown on her face
why she refused to take pictures
and why she preferred talking to me over the phone than  meeting up in person

behind that frown of her
was a crooked smile
...she never quite saw herself as beautiful because of it
I spent my days convincing her of how captivating her beauty was
and still she failed to believe victim to her insecurity

but all sad stories must end well right?
eventually she stopped frowning...I made sure of that
she stopped covering her mouth when laughing which was music to my ears
her crooked smile flashed right at me melting my heart
and I fell in love with her....I fell inlove with her crooked smile...

Why do I care?

Its not always easy to put why you love or care about a person into words
its hard not impossible but its almost like describing the taste of water
there are people who come into our lives by fate
that leaves footprints in your heart
and no matter how long you're apart their always there
inscribed in your heart like a signature
as if that's where they belong
and from the moment they enter your life
they've brought happiness because they never fail to make you laugh/smile
Its like all you've been looking for is within that person
never fail to tell you the truth no matter how much it hurts
and you may think what they say is fucked up at first
but eventually you realized they were right and they only had your best interest at heart
and you care about this person because they care about you
and because they show how much of a family person they can be
and the mere fact that your parents respect them
just draws you to that person
but sometimes we fail to show how much we care
Why though?
I guess there is no feasible excuse
but no matter how much I fail to contact you
I will always care
I care because there is no one i'd rather discuss my darkest secrets to than you
because somehow you keep them close to your heart
and you dont judge you never judge
you give advice but you dont judge me
I care because when I'm around you its like nothing else matters
I'm free, I'm young I can be myself
whether crazy, weird, blond, flirty
I am me when I'm with you
but yes all these are hard to put into words
and I hope I've done a good job in describing why I care.

Friday, 28 February 2014

She is not enough

She walks with the false idea
that she is not enough.
That among the beautiful diamonds
she's a plain rock covered in dirt.
and though they try to convince her
that her beauty is beyond enchanting
that it could leave you hypnotized.
She walks with the thought carved in the back of her mind
that she is not enough.
She remembers that one time
this boy she liked
described her in such a way
that she felt she was lacking
and though he said she was enough
...enough for someone else but not him
she failed to believe it.
Her thoughts were plagued with questions
"How can you say I'm enough
but at the same time say I'm not enough for you?"
Contradicting statement left her in dismay.
Still she walked around with the idea
believing she really wasn't enough.
that she was a plain rock covered in dirt
among the beautiful diamonds.
