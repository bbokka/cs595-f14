The first MEDLINE service designed for both health professionals and the general public.

       

Look for these PaperChase features:
  • You search over 15,000,000 references -- everything in the National Library of Medicine's MEDLINE and OLDMEDLINE databases of references to the Biomedical Literature.

  • We show you the best articles first.

  • We use time tested algorithms together with Advanced Semantic Methodology from Unveil Technologies, Inc., to map your search terms to the index terms (MeSH) used by the National Library of Medicine.

  • Our interactive searching lets you refine and combine previous search statements without starting over.

  • Verify a Reference Use this Free feature to look up a specific reference or check the accuracy of a citation.

  • Working in conjunction with the National Library of Medicine, we now feature on-line full-text of many articles. For your convenience, the full-text document link is made available for immediate download, often directly from the publisher.

  • We deliver your choice of full text articles by fax, courier, or first class mail.

  • Minimal graphics and fast-loading screens give you speedy search results.
