   More about me;

   Hi there! You've stumbled upon the thing that sort of kind of sums me
   up in a nutshell.

   Here are the basics: Cadbury-colored hair as disheveled as her mind.
   Chinky eyes. Writer of code, literature, tweets, and of her own
   destiny. Last but not the least, kawaii.

   More?

   Okay.

   I'm really passionate about slam poetry. I like reading creepypastas,
   and I like to think that I'm a very literary person. I used to write
   poetry, but now I'm trying to focus on writing prose. Give them a read
   if you have the time.

   If you happen to push the button that leads to more links of my
   accounts, make sure to hover over the dots, alright? That's where they
   are :)
   ▼

   Really gay for graphic and web design. Fonts, too! I also like the
   breakfast for dinner sort of thing. Just really enthusiastic about food
   in general. Especially my mother's cooking!

   I'm a very family-oriented person. My social life basically revolves
   around spending time with my family and friends.

   Although I find it hard to dress myself fashionably, the world is still
   my runway. It's not America's Next Top Model. It's actually Annika Is
   The Next Top Model ;) Heh. Bear with me.

   I'm very active on Twitter so if you wanna know me better, follow me
   there and we could talk maybe? Just don't be creepy? Or let's be creepy
   together?
