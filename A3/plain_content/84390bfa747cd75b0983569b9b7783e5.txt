   First Draft

   The first draft is always mind puke: senseless talk that streams from
   head to paper. Every time I’m faced with writing a draft for someone
   else other than myself I get scared. I stop what I’m doing before I’ve
   even started writing. Its crazy what I do to myself. Unfortunately,
   life isn’t always easy, and learning is another way to say ‘practice’.
   They say that a good reader helps make a great writer, but whatever
   makes a good read good?
