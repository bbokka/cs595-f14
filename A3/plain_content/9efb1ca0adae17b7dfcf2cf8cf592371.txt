About

   I suddenly feel the tides turning. A yearn for something better in my
   life. I'm hoping for big changes in my career, maybe the touch of my
   first born and the ongoing rollercoaster of my marriage.
   It feels important enough to dedicate a few minutes of my days to etch
   out how it is through my eyes... just so one day I can flick through
   and reminisce

Meet The Author

   I've spent my whole life being the son my parents wanted me to be. I
   studied hard, went through the different levels of the school system
   and then I'm now 30. I married a beautiful girl, trying to pay off a
   mortgage, and have a bucket list of things I want to do which don't
   involve work.
   I've always had a passion for blogging, photography, food, collecting
   aka organised hoarding and music and I can't think of any better moment
   to start documenting it...
