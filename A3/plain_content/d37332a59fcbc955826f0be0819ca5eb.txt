[30]How to hide posts, reblogs, and users from your Dashboard (using Adblock
Plus)

   Tumblr doesn’t have any built-in support for keeping reblogs and
   clutter out of your Dashboard, so I decided to work some in! It works
   throughout the Dashboard, and even in searches!

   I wanted to keep from writing another extension for users to install,
   and I didn’t want to use anything bulky, so I set it up using Adblock
   Plus custom filters. It’s probably possible using other page modifying
   plugins, so you can just leave me an ask if you want me to try porting
   it to something else.

   Read more for a few pre-built filters, and instructions on writing your
   own!
