[4]TruCONNECT SHOW with Benjamin 'Ace ThaEmcee' Osammor & Bimbo Johnson

   [5][picmix-192014-115124.jpeg]
   Reliable sources states that Nigerian Rapper/Enterpreneur, 'Ace
   ThaEmcee' of Beatbuxx360 & an African based online radio station,
   TruSpot FM are about to launch a new weekly radio show called
   'TruCONNECT SHOW'.
    The weekly radio program is aimed at connecting those who have
   dedicated their time, talent and resources to effect positive change in
   their life and their environment to the world
    The premier of this show will be hosted by the rapper 'Ace ThaEmcee'
   (@BEATBUXX360_ACE) & the beautiful young, fasting-rising personality
   'Bimbo Johnson' (@TheBimboJohnson).
   TruCONNECT SHOW will air every Friday morning at 10:00 a.m. beginning
   October 3,2014. Listeners are able to tune in and listen to the show by
   clicking on [6]http://truspot.fm AND/OR for Andriod devices get the
   TruSpot FM App from the Google Store or hit this link -
   [7]http://bit.ly/truspotandroid
   If you have what it takes to grace this platform follow @TruConnectShow
   on twitter, and on facebook: [8]www.facebook.com/TruConnectShow
   And start connecting.

[18]Participate in YQ’s ‘Hairstyle Art Online Contest’ for Graphic Artists
(Prize - Brand New Laptop)

   [19][ArtContest-med.jpg]
   Multi-talented singer and CADILLY Ent’s latest signee, YQ is giving
   Nigerian graphic artists an opportunity to bring their creativity to
   limelight by participating in an online competition tagged ‘Hairstyle
   Art Online Contest’ which is coined from the title of his
   soon to be released comeback single ‘HAIRSTYLE’.
   To participate in this contest simply follow these steps:
   •​Follow @iam_YQ @cadillybaby on Twitter & Instagram
   •​ Create an art with pictures of 6 female Nigerian and
   international celebrities/role-models creatively displaying their
   appealing hairstyles inscribing the hashtag #YQ_Hairstyle_Art_Contest.
   * Include YQ's picture (provided in this post)
   [20][YQ_Hairstyle_Art_Contest%2B2.jpg]
   * Use any software of your choice but export to Jpeg. Send your entries
   to
   [21]cadillyinfo@gmail.com with your NAME, AGE, SEX, OCCUPATION,
   LOCATION
   and PHONE NUMBER.
   •​Shortlisted entries will be posted on YQ’s Instagram & Twitter pages
   & the entry with the highest number of legitimate likes & retweets
   respectively, wins the grand prize of an HP Laptop with 750GB HDD &
   4GB RAM.
   Entries for this contest closes on MONDAY 8th September, 2014 @ 12
   noon & VOTING starts immediately and ends on TUESDAY 9th @ 9pm
   (Nigerian Time)
   The Winner will be announced on WEDNESDAY (September 9th).
   For more information connect with CADILLY via
   e-mail: [22]cadillyinfo@gmail.com
   Twitter/IG: @cadillybaby
   This contest is open to Graphic Artists throughout Nigeria.
