[41]Envy

   Envy may be defined as an emotion that “occurs when a person lacks
   another’s superior quality, achievement, or possession and either
   desires it or wishes that the other lacked it.”



Steps

    1.
         1. 1Steer away from envious people. They will not admit they are
            envious typically. Steer away from this person. Keep away
            until they confess and say sorry. If not keep away, you can
            not afford to keep envious people in your private world.
            People that envy you are not your friends.
         2. 2Examine his behavior. See his actions, words, etc. Sometimes
            they make face response, so be alert.
         3. 3If you would like to do something, and he says things like
            “you cant”, “you suck” or “your not going to make it” then
            those are signs of envy. For example: You would like to sing,
            and he says you shouldn’t because you are a bad singer but
            others say you have a great voice, somethings wrong.
         4. 4Try to help him out in that situation, talk to him about this
            feeling. If you dont succed try to Break Up with that friend.
         5. 5Talk to somebody about that. You might not see he’s envious,
            but if you tell others the situation they may identify it.
         6. 6Envious people talk with others about you.
         7. 7Investigate what made him that way. You might be mistaken for
            something you did that he hated and he’s trying to have
            revenge. Or maybe he’s in a bad mood, and people with bad mood
            try to make others feel like dirt.
