[13]Write up on Foxes concert

   For this show, I had more of an active role in looking after the artist
   and their team compared to the Luminites experience.
   I guided Foxes band on a tour around the pier and took them into the
   local town to buy a few items they needed. I was able to meet their
   tour manager and listened to her advice about getting into
   managing/tour managing.
   I then took them up to their dressing room and spent a good amount of
   time talking to Louisa (Foxes) and her band as well as taking some
   group photos for them.
   I was involved in supplying them with everything they needed such as
   wifi passwords, catering menu, and rehearsal times.
   I then took them down to the stage for rehearsal followed by catering.
   When it came to their performance we collected them from their dressing
   room and took them to the stage.
   The entrance onto the stage wasn’t great, so I came up with a last
   minute idea with Louisa to lift the curtain at the centre back of the
   stage so she could make a grand entrance and it worked brilliantly!
   After the show we took Louisa and her support act Ariana back down for
   a meet and greet with their fans.

   It was a fantastic night, gave me a great insight into the roles
   managers have when on the road, and reminded me of how hard I am going
   to have to work to make this my career!
