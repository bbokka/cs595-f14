   [56]nostalgicjoy

     Maybe I’m not that kind of beautiful
     dressed in red lipstick and black eyeliner.
     I’m not beautiful in the sense of
     defined cheekbones, full lips,
     curvy hips, and shapely legs.
     I’m not beautiful like the sunsets kissing the sky,
     the stars outlining evening wishes,
     the freshly picked flowers,
     and the softly murmured I love you’s.
     So maybe I’m the kind of girl
     with pale lips and dull eyes.
     But I hope someday,
     someone kisses the sunsets on my chapped lips
     and outlines the stars on my cheekbones.
     I hope he waters the flowers beneath the flaws on my skin
     and screams I love you’s because his love for me
     is meant not to be kept in secret drawers,
     but to be plastered in the sky,
     on waters, and in the wind.
     I hope he loves
     the no make up, flawed,
     clumsy, and awkward
     kind of beautiful.

   [57]on being me (NJ.)

[67]I am a poet.

   My heart and my mind linked with my fingers,
   in unison they lucidly pour out my soul on pen and paper.
   I run my fingers on the dried works of my mind, and I could swear I
   felt I was touching myself.
   I do not have to soar to the confines of the world to get a plot for my
   writing,
   in me is a universe unencompassable and in you I see a story, lined for
   a thousand pages.
   We are words in motion, all we need is a beautiful interpreter to make
   a mirror of us.
   I’m buried in notebooks of poems, engraved with specks of dust to show
   how far I have come.
   This is my life, this is me.
   I am a poet.
   -Jaylo

[109]How I hate you.

   How I hate the way you ignore me; but the love of how you pop up and
   make me smile is stronger.

   How I hate when you don’t tell me about your feelings; but those
   moments you make me believe, I love.

   How I hate when you don’t tell the world about us; but the love for
   those occasions when you show me I’m your world.

   How I hate you, for not loving me forever; but still love you for those
   times you did.
   -Jaylo
