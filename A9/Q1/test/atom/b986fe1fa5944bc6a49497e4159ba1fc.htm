Wednesday, December 16, 2009

Eargasm

I was supposed to write this yesterday, but I didn't have Internet access, so ANYWAY.

I bought Mirotic Live! Grabe, nagpa-reserve pa ako hahaha. Ang huli kong bili ng CD ay nung high school ako. Bumili ako ng Weiss Kreuz CD sa Comic Alley na kalahating taon kong pinag-ipunan. XD

May mga kasabay akong bumili na Cassies, kaso nahiya akong makipag-usap kasi mukha akong ajumma. XD

Pagdating ko sa bahay, bago pa man ako magbukas ng ilaw at magbaba ng gamit, binuksan ko yung compy at pinatugtog ang CD. Parang feel ko, nasa concert talaga ako. Pero ang ganda ng pagkarecord. Di siya maingay.

Hindi ko pa napakinggan yung mga tracks na live. Hinintay ko talaga yung release ng CD. Hahaha. Sana may DVD! Pero wishful thinking. Hahaha.

Ang mga favorite kong tracks:

- Geugeotmani Nae Saesang (Jaejoong solo) - grabe. parang yung kantang to, kinakanta sa inuman, or peyborit ng tatay mo, or pinapatugtog sa RJ. Jaejoong really showed his versatility sa track na to. At ang taray ng vocals talaga!!!!!! Nag-eargasm ako nung huling line. WAW.

- Insa - duh. Very beautiful. Number two na 'to sa top five DBSK ballads ko, next to LITI. Ang ganda talaga. Bagay sana pang-finale. Naiiyak ako.

- Poongseon remix - ah, ito inaabangan ko since I watched the Yunjae fancam hahaha. Very pretty song. Nung na-piss off ako dun sa walanghiyang singiterang ininsist pa na nauna siya, eto pinakinggan ko at napangiti ako. Mukha akong tanga sa bus. LOL

- Wrong Number - lalo na pag naiisip ko yung tutukan baril. NO TO VIOLENCE, pero men, ang hooooot nila. Eargasm starts after RESET. Jaejoong, ampogi ng pasok ng rap mo. AHLABET

Magaganda lahat ng tracks. OMNOMNOM. Pero may mga disappointing moments.

-Mirotic - UNDER MY SKY. enough said.

- Upon This Rock. ewan. hindi ko masyadong gusto ang English ni Minnie. Saka yung mga birit niya, medyo masakit. Mas OK yung Ban Dal niya na solo.

-Bakit walang picture of you saka you're my melody? Oh well.

Five stars Cassiopeia!!!!!!!! OMNOMNOM!!!!

Thursday, November 26, 2009

Somehow, it's a sad reunion

I held my breath
as I watched, I wondered
how many of us
were waiting as I did

it was like a siege
outside the walls
the hurtful words
threatened
to break us from the inside

confused
afraid
from where I was
I can hear only
the pounding
of hearts

the signal...
the beacons...
we waited for them to light
we waited for you
so that
even though far apart
we could give, united
our battlecry

and yet
when the signal came
your faces were weary
and dark with doubt
and in my heart, I wondered
have we not been strong enough?

somehow, it's a sad reunion
and when I saw you
I learned how it feels
when a heart falls and breaks

how were we supposed to
know the magnitude
of your doubt?
it was only when the beacons lit
that we saw
you have been holding
the shadow at bay
on your own
for too long

please
shine brightly again
so that we will have reason to believe
please
have faith in us as well
