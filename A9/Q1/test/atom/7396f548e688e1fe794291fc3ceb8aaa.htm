Wednesday, January 23, 2013

Hilarious Quote of the Day

So last night at dinner I was trying to talk to Dan in code telling him that I scheduled Mia's first play therapy session for Friday.  Mia started asking what we were talking about.  I didn't want to get into it right then and there.  But I told her that she is going to go talk with a really nice therapist that Daddy and I met and really like.  She is going to be like Coach Katie is for Hadley (our psychologist for Hadley).  Mia then asked "Why?".  I said well you know how you sometimes have really big fits and you don't know why you are having them.  She is going to help you to maybe understand why.

Brianna of course was all ears during the conversation and said "Ugh, Why don't I get to go?  I have a problem!"

Hilarious!!!  Go figure.  Brianna is jealous that she isn't in therapy.  Parenthood.  There is nothing quite like it.

Sunday, January 20, 2013

Our Gotcha Girl

Five years ago today, I had a restless night waiting for the phone to ring from China with news that Dan finally had our little girl in his arms. The call finally came in the wee hours of the morning and his words were "honey, I have her and she is beautiful.

If you don't believe in love at first sight , then just talk to Dan. Because he was smitten from the first moment. He walked into a room full of crying babies and saw Mia and said to himself " Lord, please let that be Mia". And it was!!!!!

We love you, baby! We are so blessed to be your Mommy and Daddy.
