Cohen's First School Field Trip!!!

I don't know who was more excited for Cohen's first Kindergarten field trip, him or me?! Oh, who am I kidding...it was me! We went to Russell Farms in Noblesville and the kids got to see farm animals, do lots of fun activities, and take a hay ride out to the pumpkin patch to pick out a pumpkin! It was so much fun, and it makes me so thankful to have a baby sitter who can watch the little kids so that I can be a part of these special opportunities at school!
