Body Language i.e. Non Verbal Communications is yet to be used as a strong tool in India. Where people still think, it is more about the body posture and how to maintain oneself, it is much more than that. Learning body language can reveal you many things which existed always in front of your eyes and you just could not see them. Micro Expression is a part of the Body Language and a vast part in itself.

What is Micro Expression?

The involuntary muscle movement of face which last very short as even 1/15th is known as Micro Expression. When we think something else about a topic and say something else about it, a micro expression flashes. It is because of this shorter time that people without Micro Expression Training cannot see it.

Different than Lie Detection

Many people think that a micro expression is a sign of deception, well , it might be, but not every time. Micro expressions tell us what the person is feeling about it not why is he feeling. To know more you have to dig deeper. It is very important that a person should be known first to gauge the micro expression.
